About HomeShakha

What are we ?


Today, Home is where we spend most of our time at. But Homes are meant for us to live, not merely exist. That's what we aim to bring back with HomeShakha. We believe Home is a place to make memories, to relax, to rejuvenate, to love and to build dreams. Above all, we want to ensure peaceful living in a healthy environment, because Home is where the Heart is right?
HomeShakha is an Apartment/Community Management Application that addresses several hassles and provides alternatives to several tedious jobs in the maintenance of residential communities.
