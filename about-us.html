About Us

Home is where your Heart is

HomeShakha is an Apartment/Community Management Application that addresses several hassles and provides alternatives to most tedious jobs in the maintenance of residential communities.

Today, Home is where we spend most of our time. But Homes are meant for us to live, not merely exist. That's what we aim to bring back with HomeShakha. We believe a Home is a place to make memories, to relax, to rejuvenate, to love, and to build dreams. Above all, we want to ensure peaceful living in a healthy environment, because Home is where the Heart is right?

We believe in the inclusivity of residential spaces irrespective of their capacity, because who doesn't deserve a great Home? Our services are available to residential spaces of all capacities to help them manage their space better. Our service bundles are curated and personal to exactly fit our customer's needs.

We're truly proud to be rolling out the app with Pandemic-specific features, that help the spaces manage infection better than ever, besides being empathetic to the infected residents and offering hyperlocal help to them in several ways, ranging from general assistance to medical emergencies.
