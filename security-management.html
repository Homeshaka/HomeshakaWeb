How it Works

HomeShakha's Security Management module simplifies communication between residents and the main gate. It enables residents to notify guards that they are expecting a visitor or delivery and guards to seek the permission of a resident when someone comes unannounced. This seemingly simple connection can bring powerful change to any community.

Features of Security Management

Visitor-Management

Don’t make your guests wait at the gate for the security to call for your approval. Approve their entry with a click or pre-approved for an even smoother entry.

Know More

Domestic Help Management

Verify your domestic help and auto-approve their entries. Be notified of in and out times of your domestic help to the Community.

Know More

Approve Deliveries

All deliveries need your approval. If you’re already expecting one, you could pre-approve it for a smoother experience. Alternatively, you could ask the parcel to be left at the gate if you’re away and would like to pick it up later.

No Unauthorised exit of Kids

Worried about the kids leaving your community while playing? You will no longer have to be so. Kids and pets cannot leave unauthorised without a guardian. In case you want your kid to go out, you’ll have to approve the same. Feel safe? We told you so.
