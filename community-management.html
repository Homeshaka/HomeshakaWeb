Features of Community Management

Security

People, pets and parcels, everything in and out needs to be authorized. No unexpected guests, no kids leaving the community, or daily help lingering around for longer than they must be. All movement is recorded and it shows up right on the resident’s app, every single time.

Know More

Maintenance

Maintenance is one of the most tedious tasks in a community. And finding help when needed could be a lot of chasing by the residents too. Alternatively, you could just raise a complaint on the app and get it addressed within a few seconds, maybe even faster than a phone call to the Help Desk? Yes, this is Future-Ready, One-Click, Help Desk for everything.

Know More

Payments

The very thought of payments in a Community can make one feel tired already. With a lot of related issues, payments are rarely smooth in a Community. To sort this out, for once and for all, the app supports paying maintenance and rents and would soon support several other payments. All Payments, bills, etc are transparent and the app does most of the accounting for you. So you just sit back and count the cash while the app does all the work.

Know More

Communication

Sharing Updates and crucial information is quite a task. Or rather Was. It’s just a click of the button now. Quick updates, notices, information, discussions, polls, you name it, we’ve got it on the app for you. Don’t worry about leaving anyone behind with the updates now.

Know More

Booking Amenities

If your Community offers Amenities like Club House or services that need prior booking/registration, that can also be done on the app. Residents can check real-time availability and book right from the app.
