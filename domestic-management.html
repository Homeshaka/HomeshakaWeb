Domestic-Management

For all the challenges you may have with Domestic Help.

Finding a maid, cook or nanny, managing their attendance and paying their salaries are cumbersome tasks. But not for homes within HomeShakha communities. Find out why.

Schedule a DEMO

Features of Domestic Management

Find Help

If you’re new to the community or dis-satisfied with your current help, you could use the Community Feature to find Help. Find new people, look at their reviews and how many neighbourhoods they work at, before you can talk to them. Call them right from the app, talk and get going. It’s as simple as that.

Manage Attendance

Don’t remember the exact number of days your cook was on leave? Don’t worry, you’ll find it on the app. You no longer have to spend time tracking attendance as the app automatically does it for you. Also, it can’t be altered, so no more discussions on this now.

Real-Time Notifications

You’ll be notified every time the maid enters and leaves the community. This is to ensure the safety of the residents and the community.

Auto-Approved and Verified Profiles

All domestic helps are verified profiles added on the application. A basic check is conducted before they are boarded onto the application. You can auto-approve your daily help and be notified when they’re in and out rather than approving day in and day out. All your daily help can be auto-approved as they’re trusted and verified.
